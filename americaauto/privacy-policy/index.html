America Auto Service & Body Shop ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and contacts. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including information collected via SMS/text messaging as part of our auto repair and body shop services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Vehicle information necessary to provide repair and body shop services
  • Mobile phone numbers for SMS communication purposes
  • Appointment and service history records
  • Communication records including SMS message history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and confirm auto repair and body shop appointments
  • Send vehicle status updates and service completion notifications via SMS
  • Provide repair estimates and service updates
  • Coordinate vehicle drop-off and pickup
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements

3. SMS Messaging

By providing your mobile number during service intake or by texting America Auto Service & Body Shop directly, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your vehicle services. Message frequency varies based on your service appointments and interactions.

Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP. Message frequency varies.

4. No Mobile Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose your mobile phone number or SMS consent information to third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third party. Your mobile number is used solely for direct service communications related to your vehicle at America Auto Service & Body Shop.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The most current version will always be available at policiesandterms.com/americaauto/privacy-policy.
