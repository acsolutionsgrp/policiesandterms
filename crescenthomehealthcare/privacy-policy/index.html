Crescent Home Health Care ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, caregivers, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including information collected via SMS/text messaging.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Health-related information necessary to provide home health care services
  • Mobile phone numbers for SMS communication purposes
  • Communication records including SMS message history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and coordinate home health care services
  • Send appointment reminders and care coordination updates via SMS
  • Respond to inquiries and service requests
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements

3. SMS Messaging

By texting Crescent Home Health Care or providing your mobile number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your care services. Message frequency varies based on your care needs and interactions with our team.

Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP. Message frequency varies.

4. No Mobile Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose your mobile phone number or SMS consent information to third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third party. Your mobile number is used solely for direct communication related to your care services with Crescent Home Health Care.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The most current version will always be available at crescenthomehealthcare.policiesandterms.com/privacy-policy. Continued use of our SMS services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
